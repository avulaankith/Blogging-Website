ABOUT US



History:

     

The Ritz Carlton has a great reputation across the United States. We have around 108 hotels worldwide and around 900 employees at each location and 8700 rooms in each hotel. This was established by William B Johnson in the year 1983. Our base location is at Bethesda, Maryland , U.S. At present Marriott International owns the Ritz hotel brand.The Ritz Carlton was awarded for its services, hotels and dining by the renowned Zagat Guide to Restaurants. Still we are trying to improve ourselves to satisfy each and every customer. We also conduct atleast 2 to 3 events every month, where our guests can gather and interact with others and form a friendly atmosphere and enjoy their food and drinks together. Our hotel also consists of children park both indoor as well as outdoor, where the parents along with their children can come and enjoy.
